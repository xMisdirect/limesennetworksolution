COMPANY PROFILE

LIMESEN NETWORK SOLUTIONS INC.is a contracting firm of well experienced and professional individuals. We offer a full range of construction services from creation of design, documentation, renovation of office buildings, government and educational institutions, finance, residential and other establishments that needs cable and infrastructures services nationwide.

LIMESEN NETWORK SOLUTIONS INC.designs and builds network infrastructure with support of multiple voice, data, video, security and multimedia system. We also offer full service industrial installation, repair and electrical custom control solutions for your organization design to fully integrate all your electrical controls and mechanical process that includes architectural and engineering drawing.

LIMESEN NETWORK SOLUTIONS INC.offer our clients all complex of general construction works and we will help you to evaluate, design and install network supports beyond client’s desired, needs and expectations. We value our customers by maintaining our client base and prized clients while building new relationship to prospective clients. We give unequalled support and incomparable knowledge to gain clients trust in partnering with our organization with the enthusiasm of our employees.
